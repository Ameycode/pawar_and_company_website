About Us

Starting a business involves various legal, financial, and administrative processes, which can often be complex and time-consuming. At M/S Pawar and Company, we are dedicated to simplifying this journey by providing a complete range of professional services tailored to meet the needs of startups and established businesses alike.

Our Services

  • Company Formation:


    We ensure a seamless company formation process, guiding you through legal compliance, business structure selection, and documentation filing with end-to-end support.

  • Income Tax Returns and Compliance:


    Our team specializes in preparing and filing accurate income tax returns, addressing compliance requirements, and providing expert advice to minimize tax liabilities.

  • GST Registration and Returns:


    We handle the entire GST process, from registration to filing regular GST returns, ensuring compliance with regulations and avoiding penalties.

  • Business Accounting:


    Maintain accurate financial records with our bookkeeping, financial reporting, and payroll management services, giving you a clear view of your business’s financial health.

  • Trademark Registration:


    Protect your brand identity with our trademark registration services, ensuring your brand name, logo, or tagline is legally safeguarded.

  • Licenses for Business Startups:


    We assist in obtaining essential licenses such as Shop Act License, MSME (Udyam) registration, and other certifications to establish credibility and ensure compliance.

With a client-focused approach and a commitment to excellence, we aim to be your trusted partner in navigating the complexities of business compliance and growth. Whether you’re an entrepreneur launching your dream venture or a business owner looking to streamline operations, we are here to support your success at every step.
